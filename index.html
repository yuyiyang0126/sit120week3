Heart Of The M4

The M4 is equited with a twin turbocharged 3.0 liters inline-six engine that comes in two potencies. It makes 473 horsepower and 406 lb-ft of torque. The Competition model makes 503 horsepower and 479 lb-ft of torque, but the lone transmission choice is an eight-speed automatic.
